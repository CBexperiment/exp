8 Ways to Prevent Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic medical condition that affects millions of people worldwide. Unmanaged diabetes may lead to blindness, kidney failure, heart disease, and other serious conditions.

Here are 8 ways to lower your risk of getting diabetes.

  • 1. Reduce your total carb intake
  • 2. Exercise regularly
  • 3. Drink water as your primary beverage
  • 4. Try to lose excess weight
  • 5. Quit smoking
  • 6. Reduce your portion sizes
  • 7. Cut back on sedentary behaviors
  • 8. Follow a high fiber diet
  • 9. Optimize your vitamin D levels
  • 10. Minimize your intake of highly processed foods
  • 11. Eat healthy plant foods
  • 12. Cook with less oil and butter
  • 13. Get regular blood or urine screening tests
  • 14. Get plenty of sleep
  • 15. Take a prediabetes risk test
